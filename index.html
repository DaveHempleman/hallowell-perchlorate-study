Hallowell Perchlorate Study

Groundwater perchlorate plume analysis (2009–2024)

Overview

This project evaluates the long-term behavior of a perchlorate groundwater plume in Hallowell, Kansas. Results indicate the plume remains spatially constrained and exhibits oscillatory behavior near the source area rather than sustained downgradient migration.
