Hallowell Perchlorate Study

Groundwater perchlorate plume analysis, Hallowell, Kansas (2009–2024)

Overview

This project evaluates the long-term behavior of a perchlorate groundwater plume in Hallowell, Kansas. The figures and downloadable data files below support the interpretation that the plume remains spatially constrained and exhibits oscillatory behavior near the source area rather than sustained downgradient migration.
